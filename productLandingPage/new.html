ABOUT US

Discover Soffty, a delightful ice cream destination nestled in Jaipur. Founded in 2023, Soffty is where ice cream dreams come to life. Our founders, Yashwant, envisioned a place where ice cream would be an art form, blending creativity and flavor seamlessly. Soffty is more than just an ice cream shop; it's a celebration of life's sweet moments. We source the finest ingredients to create a spectrum of flavors, from timeless classics to daring innovations. What sets us apart is our commitment to pushing the boundaries of ice cream perfection. We're deeply rooted in the local community, supporting farmers and businesses. Soffty's mission is simple: to make you smile. Join us in this delectable journey through the world of ice cream, where every scoop is an artwork, and every visit is a joyful adventure. Welcome to Soffty, where every scoop tells a sweet story!

VARIETIES

Vanilla Bean:

Classic and timeless, our Vanilla Bean ice cream is made with real vanilla pods for a rich, creamy taste that's pure nostalgia in every bite.

Chocolate Fudge Brownie:

Dive into a world of chocolatey decadence with our Chocolate Fudge Brownie ice cream, packed with chunks of gooey brownie and swirls of fudge.

Salted Caramel Pretzel:

Sweet meets salty in our Salted Caramel Pretzel ice cream, where velvety caramel swirls intermingle with crunchy pretzel pieces.

Cookies and Cream:

A timeless favorite, our Cookies and Cream ice cream is a blend of crushed chocolate cookies folded into creamy vanilla ice cream.

Pistachio Almond:

Nut lovers rejoice with our Pistachio Almond ice cream, a creamy pistachio base studded with crunchy almond pieces.

Toasted Coconut:

Experience the exotic with Toasted Coconut ice cream, where toasted coconut flakes add a delightful crunch to a creamy coconut base.
